Summary: Latitude vs Temperature

The analysis for this data was created on June 21 , 2018. What you are seeing is a relationship between latitude and temperature for 500+ cities in the world. As you can see, near the equator temperatures are always high, and further you are for the equater, the lower the temperature will be. Thus, the temperature of a country varies on their lattitude. For this data, the CityPy module generated mostly cities located in the northern hemisphere. Around June the northern hemisphere is closest to the equator, which leads to hotter temperatues.
