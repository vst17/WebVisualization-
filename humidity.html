Summary: Latitude vs Humdity

The analysis for this data was created on June 21 , 2018. What you are seeing is a relationship between latitude and humidity levels for 500+ cities in the world. Accoding to Sciencing.com, relative humidity is the amount of water vapor the air is holding right now as a percentage of what it would be holding if it were saturated. This graph portrays that latitude has no impact on the percent of humidity. For more information on what happens to relative humidity as air temperature rise visit Sciencing.com
